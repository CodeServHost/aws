Stenography (Shorthand) Training in Aligarh

Stenography Typing Certification Course
We train for Government and Private Sector Jobs

Anya Institute of Stenography (A.I.S.) in Aligarh conducts classes in Shorthand Stenography (Typing- English / Hindi), We are focussed on the subject and providing satisfied classes to the students. We teach Shorthand in so easy method that student can remember the story and by using their understanding skill. They get perfection in only six months.

Rajesh Sir is the Founder Director of Anya classes and he is having more than 16 years of Shorthand teaching experience. He has a huge number of successful students in UP, Who got the job of Steno in Government agencies and many more students who are doing jobs in Private and Govt. Sectors. Shorthand is very interesting subject and very demanding in Government jobs and private sectors. Shorthand is totally based on practice with quality time and interest.

Anya Steno Classes is the excellent field to work and get the job easily, every year many of seats remain vacant in different steno jobs. The requirement of stenographer is done through SSC for Indian Government. Agencies like BSF, SSC, CRPF, Defence, State and Central Secretariat, Sachiwalaya, CBI, High court as well as through UPSC, Railways and Bank etc. Learning shorthand is wise choice to get Government and Private Jobs.
